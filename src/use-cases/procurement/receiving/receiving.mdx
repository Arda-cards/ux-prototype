# Receiving Behaviors

Manages the physical flow of materials into a facility from suppliers. Covers
receiving orders against open purchase orders, lightweight receiving for
organizations without a formal dock process, the receiving list view, and
completing the purchase order lifecycle after all lines have been received.

---

### `PRO::RCV::0001` &#8212; Receive Shipment

  <tbody>
    <tr><th>Persona</th><td><a href="?path=/docs/docs-personas-keisha-receiving-clerk--docs">Keisha</a></td></tr>
    <tr><th>Status</th><td>Covered</td></tr>
    <tr><th>Depends</th><td>PRO::PO::0004, RES::KC</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

Process deliveries at the dock, marking items received and transitioning cards
to `FULFILLED`.

- **PRO::RCV::0001::0001.UC &#8212; Receive Shipment Against PO:** Navigate to
  Receiving page. POs/cards in `IN_PROCESS` grouped by supplier. Select PO or
  individual lines, click &#8220;Receive.&#8221; Cards transition `IN_PROCESS`
  &#8594; `FULFILLED`. PO advances to `RECEIVED` (all lines) or
  `PARTIALLY_RECEIVED` (some lines).

- **PRO::RCV::0001::0002.UC &#8212; Record Receiving Against PO (Lifecycle
  Stub):** Placeholder reserving the identifier. Detailed API routes not yet
  defined.

- **PRO::RCV::0001::0003.UC &#8212; Release Card to Production:** Fulfill
  action skips `FULFILLING` intermediate state by design. Physical side: pair
  received card with restocked bin, return bin to shelf location, confirm item
  is available. Future iterations may make the `FULFILLING` transition
  configurable.

- **PRO::RCV::0001::0004.UC &#8212; Receive Incoming Shipment (E2E):** On
  `/receiving` &#8220;Receiving&#8221; tab, locate item, use status dropdown
  to mark received. System fires `POST /event/fulfill`. Card moves to
  &#8220;Recently Received&#8221; tab.

**PRO::RCV::0002::0001.US &#8212; Quick Mark-as-Received:** A simplified path
for organizations that find full PO-based receiving too heavyweight.
Scan-to-receive (scan QR on package &#8594; mark received) or check-to-receive
(check a box on the receiving list without opening PO detail). Middle ground
between full receiving (PRO::RCV::0001) and skipping it entirely via
&#8220;Submit and Release&#8221; (PRO::PO::0004::0002).

### `PRO::RCV::0003` &#8212; View Receiving List

<table>
  <tbody>
    <tr><th>Persona</th><td><a href="?path=/docs/docs-personas-keisha-receiving-clerk--docs">Keisha</a></td></tr>
    <tr><th>Status</th><td>Covered</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

Browse orders pending receipt, grouped by supplier.

- **PRO::RCV::0003::0001.UC &#8212; View Receiving List (Actor-Goal):** Two
  tabs: &#8220;Receiving&#8221; (cards/PO lines in `IN_PROCESS`, grouped by
  supplier) and &#8220;Recently Received&#8221; (`FULFILLED` within 24 hours,
  grouped by supplier). Searchable and filterable by item name, SKU, or
  supplier.

- **PRO::RCV::0003::0002.UC &#8212; View Receiving List (E2E):** Badge refresh
  on page load. Search bar above list. Layout mirrors Order Queue: tabs at
  top, grouped items below.

- **PRO::RCV::0003::0003.UC &#8212; Switch Between Receiving Tabs (E2E):** Tab
  switch preserves active search filter. Allows searching for an item name
  and then checking if it has already been received.

- **PRO::RCV::0003::0004.UC &#8212; View Recently Received Items (E2E):**
  `FULFILLED` cards within the last 24 hours, grouped by supplier. Each row
  shows item name, SKU, receipt date/time, and quantity. Items older than
  24 hours must be found via the PO list (PRO::PO::0002).

**PRO::RCV::0004::0001.UC &#8212; Complete a Purchase Order (Lifecycle Stub):**
After all PO lines are received (`RECEIVED` state), a completion step advances
the PO to `CLOSED` or `ARCHIVED`. Detailed closure conditions and the
distinction between `RECEIVED`, `CLOSED`, and `ARCHIVED` are not yet defined.
