# ArdaCard

Surface card atom for grouping related content.

**Atom** &#8212; Canary | Import: `@/components/canary/atoms/card/card`

---

## Purpose

`ArdaCard` provides a rounded, bordered surface with consistent padding and
shadow. It is a composition surface &#8212; render any children inside it.
Used for detail panels, kanban card previews, and dashboard widgets.
