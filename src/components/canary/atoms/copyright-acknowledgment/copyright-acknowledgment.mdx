# CopyrightAcknowledgment

Mandatory checkbox confirming image ownership or license. Used as a gate before allowing upload confirmation.

## Baked-In Behaviors

### Controlled Checkbox

`CopyrightAcknowledgment` is fully stateless. It owns no internal checked state. The `acknowledged` prop is the sole source of truth for the checkbox state, and `onAcknowledge` is the only way for the component to report a change. If the parent does not update `acknowledged` in response to `onAcknowledge`, the checkbox will not visually toggle.

### Baked-In Legal Text

The warning text displayed alongside the checkbox is hardcoded inside the component:

> "I confirm that I own this image or have the right to use it. Uploading images you do not own or are not licensed to use may result in account termination."

This text is not a prop and cannot be overridden by the caller. It is intentionally fixed to ensure consistent legal language across all upload surfaces in the product.

### Label Association

The Radix `Checkbox` and its `Label` are linked via a shared `id`/`htmlFor` pair. This means clicking anywhere on the label text (not just the checkbox square itself) toggles the checkbox. The association is implemented with Radix primitives to ensure consistent cross-browser behavior.

### Disabled State

When `disabled` is `true`:

- The Radix `Checkbox` receives the `disabled` attribute, making it non-interactive and removing it from the tab order.
- The root container receives `data-[disabled]:opacity-50` via Tailwind's data attribute variant, reducing opacity to 50% to visually communicate the inactive state.
- The `onAcknowledge` callback is never called while the checkbox is disabled.

### Gate Behavior

The component is designed to block the confirm action in upload dialogs until acknowledged. The story below shows the full dialog context: the Confirm button remains disabled until the checkbox is checked.

## Accessibility

- The checkbox is associated with its label text via `htmlFor` / `id` linkage, so clicking the label text toggles the checkbox.
- The checkbox also carries `aria-label="Copyright acknowledgment"` as a supplementary accessible name.
- When `disabled`, the checkbox is not reachable via keyboard and the visual opacity communicates the inactive state.
