# ItemDetailsHeader

Tab bar and action grid for the item detail drawer. Up to `maxActions` actions are shown as `ArdaGridAction` cells; excess items collapse into a &#8220;More&#8221; dropdown.

**Molecule** &#8212; Canary | Import: `@/components/canary/molecules/item-details/item-details-header`

## Purpose

`ItemDetailsHeader` is a pure presentational molecule that combines two distinct zones:

1. **Tab bar** &#8212; shadcn `Tabs` driven by `tabs[]`, `activeTab`, and `onTabChange`.
2. **Action grid** &#8212; a row of `ArdaGridAction` cells limited to `maxActions` (default 6). Actions beyond the limit, plus all `overflowActions`, collapse into a `MoreHorizontal` dropdown as the last cell.
